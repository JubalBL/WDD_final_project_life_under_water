Get involved!!

Engage, act, and contribute to the protection of the ocean.

1. Reduce single-use plastics

Use reusable bottles and bags, and avoid unnecessary packaging. It’s not just about refusing plastic bags. Do you know how many microplastics you consume without even realising it?.

2. Learn where your food comes from

Do you know if your fish comes from an overexploited area? Choose products with traceability, certifications, and avoid overfished species.

3. Take part in clean-up efforts

Support projects that remove waste from beaches and help raise awareness about their importance. What local initiatives could you join?

5. Reduce your environmental footprint

Lower CO₂ emissions mean less acidification and less thermal stress in the oceans. Save energy and use sustainable transport whenever possible.
